海伯高斯管理有限公司是一家专注于中高端人才搜寻的人力资源咨询服务公司，公司专注于信息技术互联网电信行业和房地产行业，致力于为客户提供优质的中高端人才搜寻服务。 海伯高斯的顾问团队具有丰富的中高端人才搜寻经验，其核心管理团队的平均行业从业经验8年以上。公司本着“成就客户，成就员工”的发展理念，致力于为客户提供高质量的人才搜寻服务，为员工提供优质的成长平台。 海伯高斯的创始人和行业负责人曾在国内知名猎头公司科锐国际人力资源有多年的从业经验，创始人陈晶女士于在1999年进入人力资源服务领域，多年以来一直专注于该领域的长期持续发展，在服务客户的过程中不断反思、总结、提炼，以极力为客户提供更加有效益的人才解决方案。在服务该行业的多年经历中，也培养了一批优秀的招聘领域从业者。 海伯高斯重视客户的需要，致力保持我们的服务向国际水平看齐，希望通过每一步扎扎实实的工作，希望透过各顾问人员的紧密合作， 综合资源, 为客户提供最省时及合乎经济效益的人才解决方案。
Position: Intern 
Job Responsibilities
Conduct company and candidate research and analyses across industries and countries in Asia through various approaches including cold-calls Screen and assess candidates through systematic and professional interview techniques and utilizing other competency assessment tools Prepare candidate assessment reports and make professional recommendations Work closely with consultants to ensure quality, speed and accuracy of the search execution process and timely completion of assignments Assist consultants in generating revenue and driving business development
Job Requirements
Degree holder with solid academic credentials
Prospective university graduates are welcome to apply
Proactive and results-oriented with excellent people skills
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Proficient in both written and spoken English is a pre-requisite
Position: Associate Consultant/Consultant
Associate Consultant/Consultant are primarily responsible to generate revenue, establish and manage client relationships. Performance is evaluated on quantifiable measures (meeting execution and business development credits) as well as qualitative measures (revenues and yearly plans, number of completions, days to complete, completion ratio, receivables days, write off, etc.) It is important that all Associate Consultant/Consultant are to develop their recruiting practice in a specific area. Based on the Associate Consultant/Consultant' strengths, emphasis will be put on either business development or execution. Leadership potential is another important criteria.
Business Development
Work closely with the Practice Director to maintain market share in an increasingly competitive environment and improve overall operating efficiency Formulate creative and innovative marketing strategies to expose the group’s brand, develop business opportunities, and generate revenue. Maintain and establish a good relationship with existing and potential clients
Identify lists of contacts and cold-call companies for business opportunities
Perform research prior to each business development meeting
To maximize success, Consultants are to train and bring along the best team of Associate Consultants/Sr./ Research Associates to client meetings Prepare proposals and job specifications after each business meeting
Execution
Liaise closely with Research Associates to "map out" the candidate universe and prepare job control for every assignment Perform research and interview candidates
Create candidate profiles, candidate letters, candidate assessment reports based on the candidates' experience, achievements, strengths and weaknesses During the course of executive search, continuously keep in contact with existing clients by providing status reports and summaries on a regular basis When qualified candidates are shortlisted, follow up closely with the client and the lead candidate by providing reference checks as well as a detailed compensation package. It is important to manage the expectations of both the client and the candidate Manage both the clients' and lead candidates' expectations by providing compensation details, conducting reference checks, and managing the process till completion
Position: Senior / Research Associate
Sr./Research Associate work closely with Consultants on pre-search, business development support and search execution. Research Consultants are primarily responsible to performresearch and collect contact lists and details of target candidates. Research Consultants play an important role during the course of each assignments and they are the key of success.
Job Responsibilities
1. Execution
l Gather contact details based on target companies or industries given by the Associate/Principle/consultants
l Work very closely with the Associate/Principle/Consultant on candidate mapping and job control development for every assignment.
l Maintain and keep candidates/companies profile info up-to-date.
l Prepare candidate recommendation reports l Maintain search tracking on dedicated assignments
l Develop new channel and resources including social networking sites, online forums etc to enhance search efficiency
2. Conduct initial phone and F2F interviews with candidates
3. Generate professional reports and needed documents for candidate presentation. Make sure the thoroughness and consistency of all information and details in presentations
4. Manage the entire search process
l Coordinate with clients and candidates to ensure effective communication
l Assist in overall processing of the assignment with clear instructions/guidelines given by Associate/Principle/Consultant
l Pre-search/Business Development support
5. Conduct pre-search analysis including industry, market trend, target companies etc.
6. Support Business Development presentations as required by the consultants.
7. Optimize all channels and sources including internet, library, trade organizations, chambers of commerce, social networking sites for company database expansion and update.
8. Gather industry trends and market updates including positions in demand, salary and hiring trends for Associate/Principle/Consultant.
